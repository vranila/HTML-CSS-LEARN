Chapter Summary

  • form – Links input to a specific form by ID.
  • formaction – Overrides form’s submission URL.
  • formenctype – Changes encoding method for POST.
  • formmethod – Overrides HTTP method (GET/POST).
  • formtarget – Defines where results appear.
  • formnovalidate – Skips validation for that button.
  • novalidate – Disables validation for the entire form.
